About Us

Thanks for Coming By!

Bun Bun Bake Shop is a small bakery that specializes in cinnamon rolls, from our famous original gooey cinnamon roll to a variety of monthly specialty flavors.

Our shop is located in Friendship, the smallest neighborhood in Pittsburgh, PA, and we ship fresh rolls anywhere in the Pittsburgh area.

We hope our digital presence is every bit as enticing as our flavorful one.
